About Vegas-Roleplay

Vegas-Roleplay is a community built around realistic Emergency Hamburg roleplay. We aim to provide an enjoyable experience where teamwork, respect, and quality roleplay are the main priorities.

Whether you play as Police, Fire & Rescue, EMS, or Civilian, you'll find a structured community that values fair roleplay and a welcoming atmosphere.
